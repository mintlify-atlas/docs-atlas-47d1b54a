## Watch progress

Anikin tracks your watch progress locally on your device with no account required. Progress is saved every time you pause, stop, or navigate away from the video player.

Each episode is stored under a unique key in the format:

```
{mediaId}-{episodeNumber}
```

For example, episode 5 of a show with AniList ID `101922` would be stored as `101922-5`.

The saved data includes:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **WatchedDuration** | Your position in the episode, in milliseconds |
| **WatchedPercentage** | Percentage of the episode watched (0–100) |
| **AnimeName** | Name of the anime at the time of saving |

Progress is stored in `PlayerSettings.dat` in the app's local data directory.

## Progress indicators on the episode grid

When you open the episode list for any show, the app loads your saved progress for every episode in every chunk. Episodes you have started display a **progress bar** at the bottom of their card, showing the percentage watched. This refreshes each time the episode tab becomes visible.

## Favouriting a title

Tap the **heart icon** on the show's detail page to mark it as a favourite.

<Warning>
  Favouriting requires an AniList account. If you are not logged in, the app shows a "Login to Anilist" prompt. Without a login, the heart icon is visible but tapping it will not save anything.
</Warning>

Once you are logged in:

- Tapping the heart icon immediately toggles the `IsFavorite` state on screen.
- The change is sent to AniList in the background via `ToggleMediaFavoriteAsync`.
- If the AniList response disagrees with the local state (e.g., due to a race condition), the toggle is retried automatically until they match.
- The favourite state is re-fetched from AniList each time you open a show's detail page, so it reflects the latest value from your account.

Favourites work the same way for both anime and manga.

## AniList sync

Connecting an AniList account enables syncing favourites and watch progress across devices. See [AniList login](/account/anilist-login) for instructions on linking your account.

With AniList connected:

- **Favourites** are stored on your AniList profile and accessible from any device or the AniList website.
- **Watch progress** — locally stored progress is preserved on the device. Cloud sync of progress requires the AniList connection to be active when progress is saved.

<Info>
  The AniList access token is stored locally in `settings.json`. Removing the token (logging out) disables all sync features but keeps your local progress intact.
</Info>
