---
title: "Quick start"
description: "Get streaming in under 5 minutes — no account needed."
---

Anikin works out of the box. You don't need to sign in or configure anything before you can watch. Follow the steps below to go from a fresh install to your first episode.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Launch Anikin on your device. The home screen displays three content feeds:

    - **Popular** — all-time popular anime sorted by popularity
    - **Recently added** — titles with new episodes added in the last 7 days
    - **Current season** — anime airing in the current seasonal window

    Tap the feed tabs at the top to switch between them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a title">
    Tap the **search icon** in the top bar to open the search screen. Type the name of an anime or manga title and results appear as you type.

    You can also browse using the home feeds if you're looking for something to discover.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a show">
    Tap any title card to open its detail page. You'll see an overview with the show's description, and a list of available episodes below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an episode and start streaming">
    Tap an episode to begin playback. The video player opens and starts loading the stream automatically.

    If the show has both subtitled and dubbed versions, use the **Sub / Dub** toggle on the episode list screen to switch between them before tapping an episode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch providers if a source doesn't load (optional)">
    If a stream fails to load or the quality is poor, tap the **provider button** on the episode detail screen to open the provider selector. Choose a different provider from the list and the episode will reload from that source.

    See [Providers](/settings/providers) for more details on managing and switching providers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  To sync your watch progress and favourites across devices, connect your AniList account. Go to the **Profile** tab and tap **Log in with AniList**. See [AniList login](/account/anilist-login) for the full setup guide.
</Tip>
