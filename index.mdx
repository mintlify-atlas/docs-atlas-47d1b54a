Anikin is an open-source application for Android and Windows that lets you stream and download anime and manga from multiple sources. It parses content directly from providers, stripping ads for a seamless experience. No account creation is required to start watching.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Anikin">
    Grab the latest APK (Android) or ZIP (Windows) from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/jerry08/Anikin/releases).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a title and watch">
    Open the app, browse the home feed or search for a title, tap an episode, and start streaming — no sign-in needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect AniList (optional)">
    Log in with your AniList account to sync your watch progress, favourites, and media lists across devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Anikin is free and open-source. Star the [repository on GitHub](https://github.com/jerry08/Anikin) to support development.
</Note>
