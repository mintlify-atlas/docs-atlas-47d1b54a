title: "Sync and progress"
description: "How watch progress and favourites are tracked and synced with AniList."
---

Anikin tracks your watch progress in two ways: locally on your device, and — when you're logged in — through your AniList account.

## Local progress tracking

Anikin records how far you've watched each episode on your device, without requiring an account. Every episode you watch is stored in `PlayerSettings` under a dictionary keyed by `"{mediaId}-{episodeNumber}"`. Each entry holds:

- **Watched percentage** — how much of the episode you've completed (0–100%).
- **Watched duration** — the absolute playback position in milliseconds.
- **Anime name** — the title the episode belongs to.

This progress data is stored locally in `PlayerSettings.dat` inside the app's data directory. It persists across app restarts but is tied to the device.

### Progress indicator on episode cards

When you open an anime's episode list, Anikin reads local progress for every episode and displays a percentage indicator on each episode card in the grid. This gives you a visual overview of what you've started or finished without opening each episode.

<Note>
  Local progress is device-local. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, local progress is not restored unless you have AniList sync enabled.
</Note>

## AniList sync

When you're logged in with your AniList account, Anikin connects to AniList for two additional sync features.

### Favourites

Tapping the favourite button on an anime or manga page calls `AniClient.ToggleMediaFavoriteAsync`, which immediately updates your AniList favourites list. The toggle is real-time — if the AniList response disagrees with the local state (for example, due to a race condition), Anikin retries the toggle until the states match.

<Warning>
  Toggling favourites requires AniList login. If you tap the favourite button without being logged in, Anikin prompts you to sign in first.
</Warning>

### Media lists

The **Profile** tab loads your AniList anime and manga lists via `AniClient.GetUserEntriesAsync`. Entries are sorted by score in descending order. The banner image of your highest-scored title appears as a visual header for each list section.

## Cross-device sync

| Feature | Without AniList | With AniList |
|---|---|---|
| Episode progress percentage | Device-local only | Stored locally; AniList tracks list status |
| Favourites | Not available | Synced to AniList in real time |
| Media lists | Not available | Loaded from AniList on every app launch |

<Note>
  AniList tracks which titles are on your list and your list status (watching, completed, etc.), but granular per-episode progress percentages are stored locally on your device. Connect AniList to ensure your list status and favourites are available on any device.
</Note>
